# Cinnamon Rolls

Submitted by: Julia

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {12} large rolls

## Ingredients

### Dough

- {1/2} cup hot water
- {3/4} cups warm milk
- {1/2} cup salted butter
- {1} room temperature egg
- {1/2} cup sugar
- {1} tsp salt
- {4 1/2} cups flour
- {2} packets instant yeast

### Filling

- {2 1/2} stick softened salted butter
- {2 1/2} cups brown sugar
- {5} tbsp cinnamon

### Frosting

- {1/2} cup softened cream cheese
- {4} tbsp salted butter
- {3} cup powdered sugar
- {1} tsp vanilla bean paste
- {1/4} tsp salt

## Instructions

1. In a bowl or stand mixer, mix the water, milk, egg, butter, flour, sugar, yeast, and salt with a dough hook. Knead for 10-15 minutes, then place in greased bowl, cover, and rise for 2 hours.
1. While that's rising, make the filling by beating together butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon.
1. Once the dough is done proofing, flour your surface and roll into an 18x15 rectangle, spread filling, roll, cut and place in a 9x13 baking dish. This is a maximalist recipe with LOTS of filling. If you think you'll want less, use about 3/4 of the mixture. Cover and proof in a warm place for 1 hour.
1. Once the rolls are done proofing, bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
1. While those are baking, make the frosting by beating together cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla bean paste, and salt.
1. Allow the rolls to cool for approximately 5-10 minutes, frost, and enjoy!
