# Basic Crêpes

This simple but delicious crêpe recipe can be made in minutes from ingredients that everyone has on hand.

- **Servings:** {4}
- **Yield:** {8} crêpes
- [Source](https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/16383/basic-crepes/)
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 20 minutes
- **Total Time:** 30 minutes

## Ingredients

- {2} large eggs
- {1/2} cup milk, or more as needed
- {1/2} cup water
- {1/4} teaspoon salt or to taste
- {1} cup all-purpose flour
- {2} tablespoons butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Whisk eggs, milk, water, and salt together in a large mixing bowl. Add flour and melted butter; whisk vigorously until batter is smooth and pourable. If the batter is too thick to spread easily in the pan, whisk in 1 tablespoon milk at a time until it reaches the proper consistency.
1. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the pan, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crêpe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
1. Cook until the top of the crêpe is no longer wet and the bottom has turned light brown, 1 to 2 minutes. Run a spatula around the edge of the skillet to loosen the crêpe; flip and cook until the other side has turned light brown, about 1 minute more.
1. Serve hot. Enjoy!

## Notes

- **Sweet crêpes:** Whisk 2 tablespoons white sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract into the batter.
- **Blender method:** Add all of the ingredients to a blender and process a few times using short bursts until the batter is smooth.
