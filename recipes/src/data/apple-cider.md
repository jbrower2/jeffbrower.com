# Apple Cider

This apple cider recipe is easy to make at home without an apple press. Cider may be frozen for longer storage.

- **Servings:** {16}
- **Yield:** {1} gallon of cider
- [Source](https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/211772/homemade-apple-cider/)
- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 3 hours
- **Total Time:** 3 hours 15 minutes

## Ingredients

- {10} large apples, quartered
- water, to cover
- {3/4} cup white sugar
- {1} tablespoon ground cinnamon
- {1} tablespoon ground allspice

## Instructions

1. Place apples in a large stockpot; cover with water by at least 2 inches. Stir in sugar, cinnamon, and allspice. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Boil, uncovered, for 1 hour. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer for 2 hours.
1. Strain apple mixture through a fine-mesh sieve; discard solids. Drain cider again through a cheesecloth-lined sieve.
1. Serve warm or refrigerate cider until cold.
