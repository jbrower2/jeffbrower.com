# Cannoli Shells

- **Servings:** {14}
- **Yield:** {14} shells

## Ingredients

- {1 1/2} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1} tbsp Sugar
- {1/8} tsp Salt
- {1/2} cup Wine, dry
- {2} Egg White, beaten
- {0} Vegetable Oil

## Instructions

1. Into a bowl sift together the flour, sugar, and salt
1. With your fingers, work the wine gradually into the dry ingredients to form a stiff dough
1. Form into a ball, wrap in plastic and let rest at room temperature for 1 hour
1. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to 1/8-inch thickness
1. With a cutter, cut out rounds 5-inches in diameter
1. In batches, as necessary, wrap each round onto a cannoli mold, sealing the edges with egg white
1. In a deep saute pan, heat the oil to 350 degrees F
1. Fry the cannoli in batches until just golden brown and crisp, about 2 minutes
1. Drain on paper towels, let cool, then gently slip the molds from the cannoli
1. (Note: Shells can be made 3 days in advance and kept at room temperature in an airtight container.)
