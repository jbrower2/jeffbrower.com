# Maple Syrup Bread/Muffins

- **Servings:** {18}
- **Yield:** {18} muffins/1 loaf

## Ingredients

- {1/3} cup Maple Syrup
- {2/3} cup Water, warm
- {2} tbsp Dried Milk
- {1} tbsp Shortening
- {2 1/4} tsp Yeast
- {1/2} tsp Salt
- {1 3/4} cup All-purpose Flour

## Instructions

1. Using liquid measuring cup, measure maple syrup
1. Pour syrup into medium bowl
1. Using the same liquid measuring cup, measure the warm water and pour into bowl
1. This helps loosen the sticky maple syrup from the measuring cup and gets it into the bowl
1. Add dry milk, shortening, yeast, and salt
1. Stir until dissolved
1. Mix in enough flour to make a stiff dough
1. Turn dough out onto floured board and knead for about 10 minutes
1. If the dough is too sticky, add more flour, a tablespoon at a time
1. Grease medium bowl
1. Put dough in bowl and turn dough over so that the dough is lightly greased on the top
1. Cover and let rise in warm, draft-free place for about 45 minutes or until double in size
1. Punch down dough
1. Turn out onto lightly floured board and knead for about 5 minutes or until dough is smooth
1. Shape dough into loaf
1. Grease 8" by 4" loaf pan
1. Put loaf in pan, cover with clean cloth and allow to rise in warm, draft-free place for about 30 minutes or until double in size
1. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or until golden brown
1. Remove loaf from pan and let cool to warm on rack or clean cloth
