# Cherry Banana Bread/Muffins

- **Servings:** {18}
- **Yield:** {18} muffins/1 loaf

## Ingredients

- {2} Banana, overripe
- {1/3} cup Butter
- {1/3} cup Sugar
- {2} Egg
- {2} tbsp Honey
- {1} cup Cherries, dried
- {2} cup Self-rising Flour

## Instructions

1. Grease 8.5 x 4.5 x 2.5-inch bread pan
1. Preheat oven 325 degrees F
1. Mash bananas in large bowl
1. Add butter, sugar, eggs, and honey
1. Mix well
1. Mix in dried cherries, raisins, or nuts
1. Quickly mix in self-rising flour
1. Scrape batter into greased bread pan and bake at 325 degrees F for 1 hour or until done
1. Remove from oven and let loaf sit in pan for about 8 minutes
1. Turn bread out of pan and let cool on rack
1. Serve warm or cold
1. To freeze, let bread cool completely before wrapping it up and freezing
1. For muffins: scrape batter into a greased muffin tin and bake at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes
