# Brown Butter Frosting/Icing

- **Servings:** {60}
- **Yield:** {4} cups

## Ingredients

- {1/2} cup Butter
- {3} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {3} tbsp Water

## Instructions

1. In a small saucepan heat the butter over medium heat until golden brown, stirring occasionally
1. Remove saucepan from heat; stir in 3 cups sifted powdered sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla
1. Stir in enough water (3 to 4 tablespoons) to make an icing of drizzling consistency
1. Drizzle on warm cookies
