# Oreo Coal

- **Servings:** {20}
- [Source](https://www.delish.com/cooking/recipe-ideas/a69664988/best-oreo-coal-recipe/)
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 25 minutes

## Ingredients

- {48} Oreos (from {1} family-size package), divided
- {1/2} cup ({1} stick) unsalted butter
- {3 1/2} cups mini marshmallows (about {7} oz.)
- {1/4} tsp. kosher salt

## Directions

1. Place 38 Oreos in a plastic bag. Using a rolling pin or heavy skillet, crush to a fine powder. Transfer to a large bowl.
1. Scrape frosting off remaining 10 Oreos; discard (or chef’s snack!). Transfer wafers to same bag and crush to a fine powder.
1. In a large, heavy skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Add marshmallows and salt and cook, whisking constantly, until marshmallows are melted, about 1 minute.
1. Using a rubber spatula, mix in crushed Oreos from bowl (not from bag) until combined. Scrape into a medium bowl and let cool slightly.
1. Scoop Oreo mixture into balls (about 2 Tbsp. each). Roll in remaining crushed Oreos in bag. Arrange on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Let sit about 10 minutes before serving.
