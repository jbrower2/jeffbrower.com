# Red Velvet Fudge

- **Servings:** {32}
- **Yield:** {64} pieces

## Ingredients

- {4} cup Sugar
- {1/2} cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
- {1} cup Buttermilk
- {1} cup Sweetened Condensed Milk
- {3/4} stick Butter
- {2} tsp Vanilla Extract
- Red Food Coloring

## Instructions

1. Using a 8x8 pan, line pan with wax parchment paper or wax paper
1. Make sure you have some extra on all four sides
1. Combine sugar, cocoa, buttermilk, sweet condensed milk, and food coloring in a non stick pan
1. (I used a big pot) Stir to combine
1. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, but do not scrape down the sides
1. (to avoid getting crystallized sugar in fudge)
1. Using a well calibrated thermometer, make sure the fudge gets up to 238 degrees
1. Reduce heat and allow fudge to simmer
1. DO NOT STIR
1. At all
1. Like seriously dont even touch the pan
1. Allow fudge to cool to 110 degrees and add butter and vanilla
1. Beat by hand or use electric mixer until fudge loses its sheen
1. (I used an electric mixer for 20 minutes
1. Not kidding
1. Some folks say this stage takes 3-5 minutes.)
1. Place in prepared pan and allow to cool, usually 1-2 hours
