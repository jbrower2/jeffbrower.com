# Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich Banana Pudding

Created by Angela Lively.

- **Servings:** {12}
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 30 minutes
- **Chill Time:** at least 4 hours

## Ingredients

- {2-1/2} cups cold whole milk, divided
- {1} package (5.10 ounces) instant banana cream pudding mix
- {1} can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
- {1} package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
- {1} carton (8 ounces) frozen whipped topping, thawed
- {4} to {5} medium bananas, sliced
- {1-1/2} packages (8 ounces each) Girl Scout Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich cookies, divided

## Instructions

1. Whisk together {2} cups milk and pudding mix. In another bowl, beat sweetened condensed milk and cream cheese until smooth. Fold in whipped topping, then pudding.
1. Crush {6} Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich cookies; set aside. Dip {24} Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich cookies in remaining milk; place half in a 3-qt. bowl. Top with half the banana slices, then half the pudding mixture. Repeat layers. Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours. Top pudding with reserved crushed Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich cookies.
