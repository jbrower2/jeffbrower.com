# Lemon Lime Sorbet

- **Servings:** {8}
- **Yield:** {4} cups

## Ingredients

- {1} cup Sugar
- {1 1/2} cup Water
- {1/2} cup Lemon Juice
- {1/2} cup Lime Juice
- {1} Lemon Zest, whole small
- {1} Lime Zest, whole small

## Instructions

1. Heat the sugar and water in a small saucepan over medium heat
1. Cook the sugar mixture until the sugar has completely dissolved in the water, creating a syrup
1. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the other ingredients
1. Refrigerate the sorbet base until it is completely cool
1. Freeze using the directions from your ice cream maker
1. Put the sorbet in the freezer for thirty minutes to set up a little more
1. Serve the lemon lime sorbet, or store it in an air-tight plastic container in the freezer
