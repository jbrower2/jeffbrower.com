# Nana's Brownie Balls

- **Servings:** {20}
- **Cook Time:** 20 minutes

## Ingredients

- {2} sq. unsweetened chocolate
- {1} stick ({1/2} c.) butter
- {1} c. sugar or less
- {1/4} c. flour or a bit more
- {1} tsp. vanilla
- {2} eggs (unbeaten)

## Instructions

1. Melt chocolate and butter. Add rest of ingredients. Mix.
1. Put in 8x8 pan or 9-inch round pan. Bake at 345°F for 20 minutes.
1. Let cool for one hour or more, then form into balls.
1. Let set for 1/2 hour, then sprinkle with confectioners sugar and place each in a mini muffin cup.

## Notes

- **Texture:** Brownie Balls are very soft. They look undone.
