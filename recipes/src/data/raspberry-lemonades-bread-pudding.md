# Raspberry Lemonades™ Bread Pudding

Created by Kelsey Byrnes. Pudding, Ice-cream, Gelatin Desserts category winner of the 2016 National Girl Scout Cookie Recipe Contest Sponsored by Taste of Home®.

- **Servings:** {6}
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 45 minutes

## Ingredients

- {6} cups (about 4-1/2 ounces) French bread
- {1} package (8-1/2 ounces) crushed Girl Scout Lemonades™ cookies
- {6} ounces (about {1} cup) fresh raspberries
- {3/4} cup sugar
- {1-1/3} cups 2% milk
- {1/2} cup heavy whipping cream
- {2} large eggs, lightly beaten
- {2} tablespoons lemon juice
- {1} tablespoon grated lemon peel

### Lemon Sauce

- {1/2} cup sugar
- {1} tablespoon cornstarch
- {1/2} cup heavy whipping cream
- {1} tablespoon butter
- {1/4} cup lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°. Place bread cubes into a greased 11x7-in. baking dish; sprinkle Lemonades™ cookies and raspberries on top. Combine next six ingredients; pour mixture over bread cubes. Bake until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean, 35-45 minutes. Remove from oven; keep warm.
1. Meanwhile, prepare lemon sauce by combining sugar and cornstarch in a small saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in heavy whipping cream and butter. Bring to a boil; cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat; cool 15 minutes. Whisk in lemon juice. Serve pudding warm with lemon sauce.
