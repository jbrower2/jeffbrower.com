# Biscotti

- **Servings:** {42}
- **Yield:** {42} Cookies

## Ingredients

- {1/2} cup Vegetable Oil
- {1} cup Sugar
- {3 1/4} cup All-purpose Flour
- {3} Egg
- {1} tbsp Baking Powder
- {1} tbsp Anise Extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
1. Grease cookie sheets or line with parchment paper
1. In a medium bowl, beat together the oil, eggs, sugar and anise flavoring until well blended
1. Combine the flour and baking powder, stir into the egg mixture to form a heavy dough
1. Divide dough into two pieces
1. Form each piece into a roll as long as your cookie sheet
1. Place roll onto the prepared cookie sheet, and press down to 1/2 inch thickness
1. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until golden brown
1. Remove from the baking sheet to cool on a wire rack
1. When The cookies are cool enough to handle, slice each one crosswise into 1/2 inch slices
1. Place the slices cut side up back onto the baking sheet
1. Bake for an additional 6 to 10 minutes on each side
1. Slices should be lightly toasted
