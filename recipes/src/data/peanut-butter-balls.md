# Peanut Butter Balls

Gluten-free and no-bake, these buckeyes will be a hit at this year's cookie swap.

- **Servings:** {32}
- [Source](https://www.womansday.com/food-recipes/food-drinks/a29773488/peanut-butter-balls/)
- **Total Time:** 55 minutes

## Ingredients

- {2} cups confectioners' sugar
- {1} cup creamy peanut butter
- {4} Tbsp. unsalted butter, at room temp
- {1} tsp. pure vanilla extract
- {1/4} tsp. kosher salt
- {9} oz. bittersweet chocolate, chopped
- Flaky salt and melted red candy melts, for decorating

## Directions

1. Using an electric mixer on medium speed, beat sugar, peanut butter, butter, vanilla, and salt until dough forms into a ball, about 2 minutes. Roll dough into 1-in. balls and place on parchment-lined baking sheet. Freeze until firm, about 20 minutes.
1. Once balls are firm, place chocolate in a small measuring cup and microwave in 30-second increments, stirring between each, until melted and smooth.
1. Line a second baking sheet with parchment paper. Working with a quarter of the balls at a time (keep remaining frozen), dip in chocolate one at a time, letting excess drip off, and transfer to baking sheet. If desired, sprinkle with flaky salt. Repeat with remaining peanut butter balls.
1. Once chocolate has set, if desired, decorate with melted red candy melts. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.
