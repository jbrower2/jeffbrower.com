# John Mayer's Apple Cider

- **Servings:** {1}
- **Yield:** {1} batch
- [Source](https://www.instagram.com/p/Bo6LkF-nPHd/)
- **Cook Time:** 3 hours

## Ingredients

- {10} apples, quartered
- {3/4} cup white sugar
- {1} tbsp ground cinnamon
- {1} tbsp ground allspice

## Instructions

1. Place apples in a large stockpot and add enough water to cover by at least 2 inches.
1. Stir in sugar, cinnamon, and allspice.
1. Bring to a boil. Boil, uncovered, for 1 hour.
1. Cover pot, reduce heat, and simmer for 2 hours.
1. Strain apple mixture through a fine mesh sieve. Discard solids.
1. Drain cider again through a cheesecloth lined sieve.
1. Refrigerate until cold.
