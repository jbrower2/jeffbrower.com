# Peppermint Pattie-Stuffed Chocolate Cookies

This isn't an ordinary cookie dough—it's made with chocolate cake mix! It's the perfect fudgy compliment to peppermint patties.

- **Servings:** {14}
- [Source](https://www.delish.com/cooking/recipes/a45304/peppermint-patty-stuffed-chocolate-cookies-recipe/)
- **Yields:** {14} cookies
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 20 minutes

## Ingredients

- {1} box chocolate cake mix
- {1} tsp. baking powder
- {2} large eggs
- {1/2} cup canola oil
- {14} York Peppermint Pattie Minis
- Sprinkles, for decorating

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350° and line two baking sheets with parchment paper. In a large bowl, whisk together cake mix and baking powder; in another bowl, whisk together eggs and oil. Add wet ingredients to dry and stir until fully combined.
1. Using your hands, form small balls of dough. Flatten a ball, then place a Peppermint Patty in the center and fold dough over to completely cover. Transfer to baking sheet and repeat with remaining dough (you should have enough for about {14} cookies).
1. Top with sprinkles and bake until cooked through, 8 to 10 minutes. Let cool slightly on baking sheet, then transfer to a wire rack to let cool completely.
