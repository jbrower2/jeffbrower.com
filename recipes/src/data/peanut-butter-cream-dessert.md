# Peanut Butter Cream Dessert

- **Servings:** {12}
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 25 minutes
- **Chill Time:** overnight

## Ingredients

- {2 1/4} cups cold milk
- {2} packages (3.3 ounces each) instant white chocolate pudding mix
- {2 1/2} cups whipped topping
- {1} package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
- {1/2} cup butter, softened
- {1} cup confectioners' sugar
- {1} cup chopped Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich Girl Scout Cookies
- Whipped cream in a can and chopped peanuts, optional

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, whisk milk and pudding mix for 2 minutes; let stand for 2 minutes or until soft-set. Fold in whipped topping; set aside.
1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese, butter, and confectioners' sugar until smooth. Fold in pudding mixture. Spoon into an ungreased 13-in. x 9-in. dish. Sprinkle with cookie crumbs. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Cut into squares. Garnish with whipped cream and chopped peanuts if desired.
