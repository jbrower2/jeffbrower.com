# Gram's Banana Bread

- **Servings:** {10}
- **Yield:** {1} loaf
- **Cook Time:** 1 hour

## Ingredients

- {3} large bananas, mashed
- {3/4} cup sugar
- {2} eggs
- {1/2} cup butter
- {2} cups flour
- {1/2} tsp baking powder
- {1} tsp baking soda
- {1/2} tsp salt
- {2} tsp vanilla
- {8} oz chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Mix bananas, sugar, eggs, and butter with a wooden spoon.
1. Incorporate flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt into wet ingredients.
1. Stir in vanilla and chocolate chips.
1. Grease one loaf pan and bake at 325°F for an hour or longer.

## Notes

- **Texture:** Dry and more bread than cake-like. All dough into one bread pan next time. Maybe less flour?
- **Add fat:** Try two extra egg yolks to add more fat, or add {1/2} cup butter.
- **Bake time:** Bake less than an hour, more like 50 minutes.
