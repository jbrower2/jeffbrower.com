# Banana Orange Bread/Muffins

- **Servings:** {18}
- **Yield:** {18} muffins/1 loaf

## Ingredients

- {2} Banana
- {1} Orange Extract
- {2} Egg
- {2/3} cup Vegetable Oil
- {1 3/4} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1/2} tbsp Baking Powder
- {1/16} tsp Salt
- {1/4} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {1/4} cup Milk

## Instructions

1. Grease two mini loaf pans
1. Preheat oven 350 degrees F
1. Mash bananas in large bowl
1. Grate peel off one orange
1. Add peel to mashed bananas
1. Squeeze orange juice into a small bowl
1. Remove seeds and pour juice into mashed bananas
1. Mix
1. In separate bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, and salt
1. Mix dry ingredients into wet
1. Scrape batter into 2 mini loaf pans or one regular loaf pan
1. Bake for 1 hour or until inserted knife comes out clean
1. Remove loaves from oven and turn out onto rack
1. Allow to cool
1. When cool, mix confectioner’s sugar with just enough milk to make a glaze
1. Drizzle glaze onto loaves and allow to set
1. For muffins: scrape batter into a greased muffin tin and bake at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes
