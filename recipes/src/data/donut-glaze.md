# Donut Glaze

- **Servings:** {18}
- **Yield:** {18} donuts worth

## Ingredients

- {1/3} cup Butter
- {2} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {1 1/2} tsp Vanilla Extract

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
1. Stir in confectioners' sugar and vanilla until smooth
1. Remove from heat, and stir in hot water one tablespoon at a time until the icing is somewhat thin, but not watery
