# Marry Me Snickerdoodles

Submitted by: Julia - [Source](https://www.tiktok.com/@thedarlingkitchen/video/7528888204028644639)

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {8}–{12} cookies

## Ingredients

- {3 1/4} cup all-purpose flour
- {1} tsp baking soda
- {1} tsp salt
- {1 1/2} tsp cream of tartar
- {1} cup unsalted butter, softened
- {1 1/3} cup sugar
- {1/3} cup packed light brown sugar
- {2} large eggs
- {2} tsp vanilla
- cinnamon sugar mixture, for coating

## Instructions

1. Whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, and cream of tartar.
1. In a separate bowl, combine the butter, sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
1. Mix the wet ingredients into the dry until you have a cookie dough batter.
1. Form into balls and coat in cinnamon sugar mixture. Make sure to spread out on the cookie sheet.
1. Bake at 350°F for 11-13 minutes, or 9 minutes for gooey cookies. Enjoy!
