# Velveeta Hashbrown Casserole

- **Servings:** {6}
- [Source](https://cleverhousewife.com/2013/04/velveeta-hashbrown-casserole/)
- **Prep Time:** 2 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 45 minutes
- **Total Time:** 47 minutes

## Ingredients

- {1} (30 oz) package of frozen hashbrowns, thawed
- {8} oz Velveeta Block Cheese, cubed
- {1} cup sour cream
- {1} can cream of mushroom soup

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375° and line a 2.5 qt baking dish with foil for easy clean-up.
1. Gently combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl and transfer to prepared baking dish.
1. Bake for 45 minutes, until completely heated through.
