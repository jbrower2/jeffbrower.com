# S'mores Ice Cream

- **Servings:** {16}
- **Yield:** {2} quarts
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 40 minutes
- **Freeze Time:** 2-4 hours

## Ingredients

- {1-1/2} cups whole milk
- {1-1/2} cups heavy whipping cream
- {1/4} cup marshmallow ice cream topping
- {1/4} teaspoon salt
- {2} cups miniature marshmallows
- {10} Girl Scout Thanks-A-Lot® cookies, coarsely chopped, divided

## Instructions

1. Preheat broiler. In a large bowl, whisk milk, cream, marshmallow topping and salt until blended. Arrange marshmallows in a single layer in a nonstick foil-lined 15x10x1-in. baking pan. Broil 5-6 in. from heat 20-40 seconds or until golden brown. Cool slightly; freeze while processing ice cream.
1. Meanwhile, fill cylinder of ice cream maker no more than two-thirds full. Freeze milk mixture according to manufacturer's directions, adding half of each of the chopped Thanks-A-Lot® cookies and toasted marshmallows during the last 5 minutes of processing. (Ice cream maker may be very full.)
1. Transfer ice cream to freezer containers, allowing headspace for expansion. Freeze 2-4 hours or until firm. Serve with remaining Thanks-A-Lot® cookies and toasted marshmallows.
