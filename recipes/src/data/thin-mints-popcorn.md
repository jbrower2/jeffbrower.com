# Thin Mints® Popcorn

Created by Amber Szydlo.

- **Servings:** {14}
- **Yield:** {3-1/2} quarts
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Total Time:** 25 minutes

## Ingredients

- {10} cups plain popped popcorn
- {25} Girl Scout Thin Mints® cookies, coarsely chopped, divided
- {1-1/2} cups white baking chips
- {1} teaspoon shortening
- {1} cup Andes creme de menthe baking chips

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine popcorn and half of the crushed Thin Mints® cookies. In a microwave, melt baking chips with shortening; stir until smooth. Pour over popcorn mixture; toss to coat.
1. Immediately spread onto waxed paper; sprinkle with remaining crushed Thin Mints® cookies. Melt baking chips in microwave; stir until smooth. Drizzle over popcorn; let stand until set.
1. Break into pieces. Store in an airtight container.
