# Texas Cinnamon Butter

- **Servings:** {8}
- [Source](https://www.delish.com/cooking/recipe-ideas/recipes/a54121/texas-cinnamon-butter-recipe/)
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Ingredients

- {2} sticks ({1} cup) butter, softened
- {1/4} cup honey
- {1} tsp. ground cinnamon, plus more for sprinkling
- {1} tsp. kosher salt
- {1/2} tsp. pure vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients. Using a hand mixer, beat all ingredients until fully combined and butter is slightly whipped.
1. Place butter in a ramekin and garnish with a sprinkle of cinnamon. Slather on everything.
