# Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich Candy Bars

Created by Kate Jackette.

- **Servings:** {36}
- **Yield:** {3} dozen bars
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 10 minutes

## Ingredients

- {3/4} teaspoon vanilla extract
- {1/2} teaspoon baking soda
- {3/4} cup plus {3} tablespoons sugar
- {3/4} cup corn syrup
- {2} tablespoons water
- {1-1/2} cups natural peanut butter
- {1} package (8 ounces) chopped Girl Scout Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich cookies, divided
- {3/4} cup plus {2} tablespoons 60% cacao bittersweet chocolate baking chips, divided

## Instructions

1. Line a 13x9-in. pan with parchment paper. Combine vanilla and baking soda.
1. In a large saucepan over high heat, stir together sugar, corn syrup and water until sugar is dissolved. Reduce heat to medium-high; cook until a thermometer reads 285°. Immediately add vanilla mixture.
1. Meanwhile, microwave peanut butter and all but {1/2} cup chopped Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich cookies until hot, about 2 minutes, stirring once. Quickly pour caramel into peanut butter mixture and stir just to combine. Immediately pour into prepared pan; spread evenly (candy sets quickly).
1. Top with {3/4} cup baking chips, reserving {2} tablespoons; spread to cover. Sprinkle with remaining chopped Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwich cookies. Melt reserved baking chips; drizzle over top. When cool, cut into bars.
