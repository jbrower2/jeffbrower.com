# Key Lime Pie

- **Servings:** {8}
- **Yield:** {1} pie

## Ingredients

- {28} oz Condensed Milk
- {1} cup Lime Juice
- {2} Egg
- {1} tbsp Lime Zest
- {1} Pie Crust, baked

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F
1. Combine the condensed milk, lime juice, and eggs
1. Whisk until well blended and place the filling in the cooled pie shell
1. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes and allow to chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours
1. Sprinkle the lime zest as a garnish and serve chilled
