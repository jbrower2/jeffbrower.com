# Caramel deLites®/Samoas® Swirl No-Churn Ice Cream

Created by Peggie Brott.

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {1-1/2} quarts
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
- **Freeze Time:** overnight

## Ingredients

- {2} cups heavy whipping cream
- {2} cups cream of coconut
- {12} Girl Scout Caramel deLites®/Samoas® cookies, coarsely chopped
- {1/2} cup flaked coconut, toasted
- {1} cup caramel sundae syrup

## Instructions

1. Beat heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold in cream of coconut, then chopped Caramel deLites®/Samoas® cookies and toasted coconut. Spread evenly over an 8-in. square baking pan. Spoon caramel syrup over cream mixture; swirl with a knife.
1. Freeze, covered, overnight.

## Notes

- **To toast coconut:** bake in a shallow pan in a 350° oven for 5-10 minutes or cook in a skillet over low heat until golden brown, stirring occasionally.
