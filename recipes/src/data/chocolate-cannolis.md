# Chocolate Cannolis

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {12} cannolis

## Ingredients

- {15} oz Ricotta Cheese
- {1/4} cup Sugar
- {4} tsp Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {1/2} tsp Orange Zest
- {1/4} cup Bittersweet Chocolate, chopped
- {12} Cannoli Shells

## Instructions

1. For filling, in a medium bowl stir together ricotta cheese, granulated sugar, 4 teaspoons cocoa powder, vanilla, and orange peel until almost smooth
1. Fold in chocolate
1. Cover and chill until needed
1. Spoon filling into a pastry bag fitted with a large open star or round tip
1. Pipe filling into cannoli shells
1. If desired, cover and chill for up to 1 hour
1. Before serving, sprinkle cannoli with powdered sugar and, if desired, additional cocoa powder
