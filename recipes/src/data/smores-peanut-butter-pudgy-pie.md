# S'mores Peanut Butter Pudgy Pie

- **Servings:** {1}
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Ingredients

- {2} slices cinnamon bread
- {2} tablespoons Nutella
- {1} Girl Scout Peanut Butter Patties®/Tagalongs®, crumbled
- {1} tablespoon miniature marshmallows

## Instructions

1. Place one slice of bread in a greased sandwich iron. Spread with Nutella; sprinkle with crumbled Girl Scout Peanut Butter Patties®/Tagalongs® and marshmallows. Top with remaining bread slice. Close iron.
1. Cook over a hot campfire for 1 to 1-1/2 minutes or until golden brown, turning occasionally.
