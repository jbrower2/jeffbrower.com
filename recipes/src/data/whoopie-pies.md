# Whoopie Pies

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {12} pies

## Ingredients

- {2} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1/2} cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
- {1/2} cup Water, hot
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {1} tsp Baking Soda
- {1} cup Sugar
- {2} stick Butter
- {1} Egg

## Instructions

1. Cream together 1 cup of the butter or margarine and the egg
1. Add 1 cup white sugar, vanilla and hot water
1. Stir in the flour, cocoa, and baking soda and mix well
1. Drop from a teaspoon onto cookie sheets and bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 10 to 12 minutes
1. Sandwich cookies together with Peanut Butter Frosting
