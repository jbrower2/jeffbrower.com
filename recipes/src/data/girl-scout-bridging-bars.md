# Girl Scout Bridging Bars

Created by Daniel Rinaldi.

- **Servings:** {36}
- **Yield:** {3} dozen bars
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 25 minutes
- **Chill Time:** at least 4 hours

## Ingredients

- {1-1/2} cups chunky peanut butter
- {1/4} cup canola oil
- {14} ounces milk chocolate, melted
- {1} package (8 ounces) Girl Scout Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwiches cookies, coarsely crushed
- {2-1/2} cups heavy whipping cream
- {12} ounces semisweet chocolate, melted and cooled slightly
- {1} tablespoon baking cocoa

## Instructions

1. Line a 13x9-in. pan with parchment paper, letting ends extend up sides.
1. In a large bowl, beat peanut butter and oil until blended. Add melted milk chocolate and crushed Do-si-dos®/Peanut Butter Sandwiches cookies. Spread into prepared pan. Refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.
1. In a large bowl, beat cream until soft peaks form. Whisk a third of the whipped cream into melted semisweet chocolate until smooth and shiny; fold mixture into remaining whipped cream. Spread over cookie layer. Refrigerate, covered, at least 4 hours or overnight.
1. Just before serving, dust with baking cocoa. Lifting with parchment paper, remove from pan. Cut into bars.
