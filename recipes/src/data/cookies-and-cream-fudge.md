# Cookies and Cream Fudge

- **Servings:** {32}
- **Yield:** {64} squares

## Ingredients

- {20} oz White Chocolate, chunks
- {14} oz Condensed Milk
- {1} Oreos, package

## Instructions

1. Break up Oreos
1. Prepare a 9x9 inch pan
1. Simply pop your condensed milk and white chocolate into a bowl over a pot of water and heat gently
1. Stir occasionally until its all melted and amalgamated
1. Take off the heat , add in the Oreos and stir gently
1. Pour into your prepared pan
1. This mix is a little thick so you may need to give it a hand into the corners
1. Pop into the fridge for 2 hours or over night to set
1. Cut into squares and serve
