# Cornbread/Muffins

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {12} muffins/2/3 loaf

## Ingredients

- {1} cup Cornmeal
- {1} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1} tbsp Baking Powder
- {1/2} cup Sugar
- {1} tsp Salt
- {1} cup Milk, whole
- {2} Egg
- {1/2} stick Butter
- {1/4} cup Honey

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
1. Into a large bowl, mix the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt
1. In another bowl, whisk together the whole milk, eggs, butter, and honey
1. Add the wet to the dry ingredients and stir until just mixed
1. Place muffin paper liners in a 12-cup muffin tin
1. Evenly divide the cornbread mixture into the papers
1. Bake for 15 minutes, until golden
