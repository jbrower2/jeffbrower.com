# Frank's Red Hot Chicken Cutlets

- **Servings:** {4}

## Ingredients

- {20} oz thin sliced chicken breasts
- {1/2} cup panko breadcrumbs
- {1/2} oz Frank's seasoning blend
- sour cream

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 420 F
1. Pat chicken dry with paper towels; season with salt and pepper
1. Combine panko breadcrumbs, Frank's seasoning blend, 2 tbsp melted butter, and a pinch of salt and pepper
1. Place the chicken in an oven-safe dish with sides, and brush a thin coating of sour cream on top
1. Cover the top of the chicken cutlets with the breadcrumb mixture
1. Roast chicken on middle rack until browned and cooked through, about 15-18 minutes
