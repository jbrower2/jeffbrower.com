# White Chocolate Cheesecake

- **Servings:** {16}
- **Yield:** {1} cheesecake

## Ingredients

- {2} cup White Chocolate, chopped
- {1/2} cup Half-and-half Cream
- {24} oz Cream Cheese, softened
- {1/2} cup Sugar
- {3} Egg
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C)
1. In a metal bowl over a pan of simmering water, melt white chocolate chips with half-and-half, stirring occasionally until smooth
1. In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese and 1/2 cup sugar until smooth
1. Beat in eggs one at a time
1. Blend in vanilla and melted white chocolate
1. Pour over crust
1. Bake for 55 to 60 minutes, or until filling is set
1. Cool, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 8 hours before removing from pan
