# Gingerbread

## Ingredients

- {1/3} cup shortening
- {1} cup brown sugar
- {1 1/2} cups dark molasses
- {2/3} cup cold water
- {6} cups sifted flour
- {2} tsp baking soda
- {1} tsp salt
- {1} tsp allspice
- {1} tsp ginger
- {1} tsp cloves
- {1} tsp cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Mix thoroughly shortening, brown sugar, and dark molasses.
1. Stir in cold water.
1. Sift together flour, baking soda, salt, allspice, ginger, cloves, and cinnamon, and stir in.
1. Bake about 15 minutes at 350°F.
