# Orange Julius

- **Servings:** {2}
- [Source](https://www.reddit.com/r/Old_Recipes/comments/11sv2iv/orange_julius_finally/)

## Ingredients

- {3} navel oranges, rind removed, quartered
- {1/2} cup whole milk
- {1/4} cup sugar
- {1} teaspoon vanilla extract
- {1/3} cup frozen orange juice concentrate
- ice

## Instructions

1. In a blender, add oranges, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, frozen orange juice concentrate and blend until well combined.
1. Add {1} cup of ice and blend well.
