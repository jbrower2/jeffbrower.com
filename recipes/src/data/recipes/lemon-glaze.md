# Lemon Glaze

- **Servings:** {44}
- **Yield:** {44} cookies

## Ingredients

- {1 1/2} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {3} tbsp Lemon Juice
- {1} Lemon Zest, (whole lemon)

## Instructions

1. Combine the powdered sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest in a small bowl and stir until smooth
1. Spoon about 1/2-teaspoon onto each cookie and use the back of the spoon to gently spread
1. Let the glaze harden for about 2 hours
