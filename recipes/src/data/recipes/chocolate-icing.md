# Chocolate Icing

- **Servings:** {8}
- **Yield:** {2} cups

## Ingredients

- {1/2} stick Butter
- {1/2} cup Brown Sugar
- {2} tbsp Milk
- {1 1/2} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {1} tbsp Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract

## Instructions

1. In a saucepan, melt butter and brown sugar over medium heat
1. Stir until sugar is dissolved, then add milk
1. Bring to a boil and remove from heat
1. Sift together cocoa and confectioners sugar
1. Blend into butter mixture and add vanilla
1. If consistency is too stiff, add more milk
1. Spread Quickly over cooled cake, as frosting will set up very fast
