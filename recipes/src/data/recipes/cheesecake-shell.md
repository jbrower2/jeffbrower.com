# Cheesecake Shell

- **Servings:** {16}
- **Yield:** {1} shell

## Ingredients

- {1 1/2} cup Graham Crackers, crumbs or 1 1/2 cup Oreos, cookie crumbs or 1 1/2 cup Cannoli Shells, crushed
- {1/4} stick Butter
- {2} tbsp Sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F ( 175 degrees C)
1. Lightly grease a 9 inch springform pan
1. For oreos: In a small bowl, mix together crumbs, sugar, and melted butter
1. For graham crackers or cannolis: In a small bowl, mix together crumbs and melted butter (omit sugar)
1. Press onto the bottom and 1 1/2 inch up the sides of the prepared 9 inch springform pan
1. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 10 minutes
1. Allow to cool
