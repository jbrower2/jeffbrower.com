# Chocolate Cannoli Shells

- **Servings:** {32}
- **Yield:** {32} cannoli shells

## Ingredients

- {2} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1/2} cup Sugar
- {1/2} cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
- {1/8} tsp Salt
- {1/4} cup Shortening
- {2} Egg
- {1/4} cup Milk
- {2} tbsp Honey
- {1} Egg White

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together all-purpose flour, sugar cocoa powder, and salt
1. Using a pastry blender, cut in shortening into flour mixture until mixture resembles coarse crumbs
1. In a small bowl, stir together eggs, milk, and honey
1. Add egg mixture to flour mixture
1. Stir just until mixture forms a ball
1. Divide dough in half
1. On a lightly floured surface, roll each dough portion into a 16-inch square
1. Cut each square into sixteen 4-inch squares
1. Wrap each square lightly around one end of greased metal cannoli cylinders
1. In a small bowl, lightly beat 1 egg white
1. Moisten overlapping dough with egg white; press gently to seal
1. Fry cannoli shells, a few at a time, in deep, hot vegetable oil (350 degrees F) about 1 minute or until golden brown
1. Using tongs, carefully and gently lift shells from hot oil; drain any hot oil that is in metal cannoli cylinders back into pan
1. Drain on paper towels
1. Cool
1. Repeat with remaining squares
