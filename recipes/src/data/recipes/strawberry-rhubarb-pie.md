# Strawberry Rhubarb Pie

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {1} pie

## Ingredients

- {2 1/2} cup Rhubarb, fresh and chopped
- {2 1/2} cup Strawberries, chopped
- {1 1/2} cup Sugar
- {2} tbsp Tapioca
- {1} tbsp All-purpose Flour
- {1/2} tsp Lemon Zest
- {1/2} tsp Lemon Juice
- {1/2} tsp Cinnamon
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {3} tbsp Butter, cubed
- {1} Egg White
- {1} tsp Water
- {2} Pie Crust, unbaked

## Instructions

1. Mix the rhubarb, strawberries, sugar, tapioca, flour, zest and juice of lemon, dash of cinnamon, and vanilla
1. Mix well in a large bowl and pour out into chilled crust
1. Dot the top of the filling with the butter
1. Brush edges of pie crust with egg white wash
1. Roll out the other piece of dough and place over filling
1. Crimp to seal edges
1. Beat egg white with water
1. Brush with egg white wash and garnish with large granule sugar
1. Collar with foil and bake at 425 degrees F for 15 minutes
1. Decrease temperature to 375 degrees F and bake for an additional 45 to 50 minutes, or until the filling starts bubbling
1. Higher altitude will take 450 degrees F and 400 degrees F respectively
1. Also, you can use a pie bird for extra decor
1. Let cool before serving
