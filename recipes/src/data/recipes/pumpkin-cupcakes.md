# Pumpkin Cupcakes

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {12} cupcakes

## Ingredients

- {1} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1} tsp Cinnamon
- {1/2} tsp Ginger
- {1/2} tsp Allspice
- {1/2} tsp Nutmeg
- {1/2} tsp Baking Powder
- {1/2} tsp Baking Soda
- {1/4} tsp Salt
- {1} stick Butter
- {1/2} cup Sugar
- {1/2} cup Canned Pumpkin
- {1 1/2} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {2} Egg

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
1. Line 1 (12-cup) muffin pan with paper cupcake liners
1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cinnamon, ginger, allspice, nutmeg, baking powder, baking soda and 1/4 teaspoon salt
1. Set aside
1. In a large bowl, add the butter and the sugar
1. Beat with a hand-held mixer until light and fluffy, 3 to 4 minutes
1. Add the pumpkin puree and the vanilla extract
1. Slowly beat in the eggs, one at a time
1. Incorporate the dry mixture into the wet mixtures together until thoroughly combined
1. Using an ice cream scoop, fill each cupcake liner 3/4 of the way full
1. Bake until the tops turn golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center of the cupcake comes out clean, about 20 minutes
1. Remove the cupcakes from the oven to a wire rack and cool completely before frosting
