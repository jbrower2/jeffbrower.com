# Apple/Pear Pie

- **Servings:** {8}
- **Yield:** {1} pie

## Ingredients

- {5} Apple or 5 Pear
- {3/4} cup Sugar
- {2} tbsp All-purpose Flour
- {1} tsp Cinnamon
- {2} Pie Crust, unbaked

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
1. Peel, core, and slice apples
1. Mix apple slices with sugar, flour, and cinnamon
1. Place apple mixture in pie shell
1. Place second pie crust on top and form to the edges of the pie plate
1. Cut slits in the top crust to allow for steam to release in oven
1. Bake for approximately 30 to 40 minutes, until pie is fully baked and apples are tender
