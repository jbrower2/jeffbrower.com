# Peach Pie

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {1} pie

## Ingredients

- {10} Peach, ripe
- {1} Lemon, juiced
- {1/2} tsp Almond Extract
- {2/3} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1} cup Sugar
- {1/2} tsp Cinnamon
- {1/8} tsp Nutmeg
- {1/2} tsp Salt
- {3} tbsp Butter
- {1} Egg
- {2} Pie Crust, unbaked for top and bottom

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F
1. Peel and cut the peaches into 1/4-inch thick slices (you should have about 7 cups) and toss with the lemon juice and almond extract in a large bowl
1. Whisk the flour, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt in another bowl, making sure there are no lumps
1. Stir the flour mixture into the peaches and mix well
1. Pour the peaches into the pie crust
1. Scatter with the pieces of butter
1. Top with the lattice pie shell strips
1. Brush away any flour underneath the lattice to make sure it seals well
1. Crimp the edges to seal
1. Beat the egg with 1 teaspoon water and brush the lattice and edges with the egg wash
1. Pop in the oven and bake for 10 minutes, and then lower the heat to 350 degrees F and bake until the crust gets brown, about 50 minutes more
1. If the edges brown too fast, cover them with strips of aluminum foil about halfway through baking
1. Cool about 30 minutes before serving
