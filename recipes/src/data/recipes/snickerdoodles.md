# Snickerdoodles

- **Servings:** {20}
- **Yield:** {20} cookies

## Ingredients

- {2 3/4} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1} tsp Baking Soda
- {1/2} tsp Salt
- {1/2} cup Shortening
- {1} stick Butter, softened
- {1 1/2} cup Sugar, plus 3 tablespoons
- {2} Egg
- {1} tbsp Cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
1. Sift the flour, baking soda, and salt into a bowl
1. With a handheld or standing mixer, beat together the shortening and butter
1. Add the 1 1/2 cups sugar and continue beating until light and fluffy, about 5 minutes
1. Add the eggs, 1 at a time, beating well after each addition
1. Add the flour mixture and blend until smooth
1. Mix the 3 tablespoons sugar with the cinnamon in a small bowl
1. Roll the dough, by hand, into 1 1/2-inch balls
1. Roll the balls in the cinnamon sugar
1. Flatten the balls into 1/2-inch thick disks, spacing them evenly on unlined cookie sheets
1. Bake until light brown, but still moist in the center, about 12 minutes
1. Cool on a rack
