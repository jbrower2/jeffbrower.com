# Tiramisu

- **Servings:** {6}
- **Yield:** {6} servings

## Ingredients

- {6} Egg Yolk
- {3} tbsp Sugar
- {1} lb Mascarpone Cheese
- {1 1/2} cup Espresso, strong and cooled
- {2} tsp Rum, dark
- {24} Ladyfingers
- {1/2} cup Bittersweet Chocolate Shavings, for garnish

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, using an electric mixer with whisk attachment, beat egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale, about 5 minutes
1. Add mascarpone cheese and beat until smooth
1. Add 1 tablespoon of espresso and mix until thoroughly combined
1. In a small shallow dish, add remaining espresso and rum
1. Dip each ladyfinger into espresso for only 5 seconds
1. Letting the ladyfingers soak too long will cause them to fall apart
1. Place the soaked ladyfinger on the bottom of a 13 by 9 inch baking dish, breaking them in half if necessary in order to fit the bottom
1. Spread evenly 1/2 of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers
1. Arrange another layer of soaked ladyfingers and top with remaining mascarpone mixture
1. Cover tiramisu with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, up to 8 hours
1. Before serving, sprinkle with chocolate shavings
