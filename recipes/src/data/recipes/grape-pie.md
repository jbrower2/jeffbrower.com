# Grape Pie

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {1} pie

## Ingredients

- {1} Pie Crust, baked
- {1} lb Grapes, halved
- {1/2} cup Honey
- {2} tbsp Brandy
- {2} tbsp Lime Juice
- {16} oz Sour Cream
- {1/2} cup Brown Sugar
- {1} tbsp Vanilla Extract
- {3/4} cup Brown Sugar
- {6} tbsp Butter
- {1/2} tsp Salt

## Instructions

1. Combine the grapes, honey, brandy and lime juice in a zip-top bag, seal and refrigerate for 8 to 12 hours
1. Whisk the sour cream, 1/2 cup brown sugar and vanilla together in a large mixing bowl
1. Drain the grapes and reserve the liquid for another use
1. Add the grapes to the sour cream mixture and stir gently to combine
1. Pour the grape mixture on top of the crust and set aside
1. Combine the 3/4 cup brown sugar and butter in a 1-quart saucier and place over medium-high heat
1. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly
1. Stir constantly and boil for 1 minute
1. Remove from the heat and pour over the grape mixture
1. Do not stir into the sour cream mixture
1. Cool at room temperature for 5 minutes, and then sprinkle the sea salt on top
1. Chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour before slicing
