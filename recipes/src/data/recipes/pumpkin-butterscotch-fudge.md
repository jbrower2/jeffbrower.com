# Pumpkin Butterscotch Fudge

- **Servings:** {64}
- **Yield:** {128} pieces

## Ingredients

- {2} cup Sugar
- {1} cup Brown Sugar
- {3/4} cup Butter
- {2/3} cup Evaporated Milk
- {1/2} cup Canned Pumpkin
- {2} tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice
- {7} oz Marshmallow Cream
- {11} oz Butterscotch Morsels

## Instructions

1. Line a 9x13 inch baking pan with foil
1. In a heavy pot combine sugar, brown sugar, milk, pumpkin, butter and spice
1. Bring to a full boil and stir constantly
1. Continue for 12 to 15 minutes until candy thermometer reached soft-ball stage, 234 to 240 degrees F
1. Remove from heat and quickly add in butterscotch, marshmallow and vanilla
1. Stir until butterscotch is completely melted and then add to pan
1. Allow to cool for at least 2 hours
1. Cut into 1 inch squares, removing the foil, and share with good friends!
1. Store in an air tight container in the fridge
1. This fudge was very sweet! I made it with and without the walnuts
1. The walnuts helped to bring the sweetness down a bit
1. If you like super sweet fudge, this is the treat for you!
