# Molasses Cookies

- **Servings:** {24}
- **Yield:** {24} cookies

## Ingredients

- {1} cup Brown Sugar, packed
- {3/4} cup Shortening
- {1/4} cup Molasses
- {1} Egg
- {2 1/4} cup All-purpose Flour
- {2} tsp Baking Soda
- {1} tsp Cinnamon
- {1} tsp Ginger
- {1/2} tsp Cloves
- {1/4} tsp Salt
- {3} tbsp Sugar

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 325°F
1. In large bowl, beat brown sugar, shortening, molasses and egg with electric mixer on medium speed, or mix with spoon
1. Stir in remaining ingredients except granulated sugar
1. Shape dough by rounded tablespoonfuls into 1 1/2-inch balls
1. Dip tops into granulated sugar
1. On ungreased cookie sheet, place balls, sugared sides up, about 2 inches apart
1. Bake 13 to 16 minutes or just until set and cookies appear dry
1. Immediately remove from cookie sheet to cooling rack
