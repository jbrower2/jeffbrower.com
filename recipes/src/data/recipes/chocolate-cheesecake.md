# Chocolate Cheesecake

- **Servings:** {16}
- **Yield:** {1} cheesecake

## Ingredients

- {1/4} cup Heavy Cream
- {1/4} cup Semisweet Chocolate, chopped
- {24} oz Cream Cheese
- {1} cup Sugar
- {1/3} cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
- {3} Egg
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract

## Instructions

1. In a small saucepan, heat 1/4 cup whipping cream and 1/4 cup chocolate chips, stirring constantly, until chips are melted
1. Remove from heat
1. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and 1 cup of sugar until smooth
1. Add cocoa and beat well
1. Add eggs and beat on low until just blended
1. Stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla and reserved chocolate mixture until blended
1. Pour over crust
1. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 45 to 50 minutes or until center is almost set
