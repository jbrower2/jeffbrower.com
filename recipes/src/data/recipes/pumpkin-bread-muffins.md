# Pumpkin Bread/Muffins

- **Servings:** {18}
- **Yield:** {18} muffins/1 loaf

## Ingredients

- {2} cup All-purpose Flour
- {2} tsp Cinnamon
- {1} tsp Baking Soda
- {1/4} tsp Baking Powder
- {1/2} tsp Salt
- {1 1/2} cup Sugar
- {3/4} cup Vegetable Oil
- {3} Egg
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {3} cup Shredded Pumpkin
- {1} cup Pumpkin Seeds, toasted

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F
1. Sift the flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together
1. In a separate bowl, mix the sugar, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla
1. Combine both mixtures and fold in the shredded pumpkin and pumpkin seeds
1. Once the ingredients are all incorporated pour into a non- stick 9 by 5 by 3-inch loaf pan
1. If your pan is not non- stick coat it with butter and flour
1. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes
1. At this point a knife inserted into the middle of the loaf should come out clean
1. Cool for 15 minutes and turn out onto a cooling rack
1. Cool completely
1. For muffins temperature should also be 325 degrees F., but bake for 30 minutes
