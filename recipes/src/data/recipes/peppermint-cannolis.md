# Peppermint Cannolis

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {12} cannolis

## Ingredients

- {1} cup Mascarpone Cheese
- {2} tbsp Sugar
- {2} tbsp Candy Canes, crushed

## Instructions

1. Fill a pastry bag fitted with a 1/2-inch plain or star tip, or a ziplock bag, with the peppermint filling
1. If using a ziplock bag, cut about 1/2 inch off one corner
1. Insert the tip in the cannoli shell and squeeze gently until the shell is half filled
1. Turn the shell and fill the other side
1. You can also use a teaspoon to do this, although it’s messier and will take longer
