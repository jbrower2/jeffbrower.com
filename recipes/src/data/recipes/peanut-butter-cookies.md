# Peanut Butter Cookies

- **Servings:** {24}
- **Yield:** {24} cookies

## Ingredients

- {1/2} cup Sugar
- {1/2} cup Brown Sugar, packed
- {1/2} cup Butter, room temperature
- {1/2} cup Peanut Butter
- {1} Egg
- {1 1/4} cup All-purpose Flour
- {3/4} tsp Baking Soda
- {1/2} tsp Baking Powder
- {1/4} tsp Salt

## Instructions

1. Beat the butter until creamy, 2 minutes
1. Add the sugars, beat for 2 more minutes
1. Mix in the peanut butter and egg
1. Mix together the dry ingredients - flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt
1. Stir the dry ingredients into the sugar butter mixture
1. Wrap dough in plastic and refrigerate at least 3 hours
1. Preheat oven to 375°F
1. Shape dough into 1 1/4 inch balls
1. Place about 3 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet
1. Flatten in crisscross pattern with a fork
1. Bake until light brown, 9 to 10 minutes
1. Cool on baking sheets for a minute; transfer to rack to cool completely
1. For chewier cookies, bake at 300°F for 15 minutes
