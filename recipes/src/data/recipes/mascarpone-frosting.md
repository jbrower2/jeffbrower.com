# Mascarpone Frosting

- **Servings:** {18}
- **Yield:** {1 1/2} cups

## Ingredients

- {3} oz Cream Cheese
- {3/4} stick Butter
- {1/3} cup Mascarpone Cheese
- {3} tbsp Honey

## Instructions

1. Using an electric mixer, beat the cream cheese and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy
1. Beat in the mascarpone cheese and then beat in the honey
