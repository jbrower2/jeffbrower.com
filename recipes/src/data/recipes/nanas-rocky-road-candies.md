# Nana's Rocky Road Candies

- **Servings:** {36}
- **Yield:** {36} candies

## Ingredients

- {14} oz Semisweet Chocolate
- {6} tbsp Peanut Butter
- {1} cup Mini Marshmallows
- {1} cup Peanuts
- {1} cup Rice Krispies

## Instructions

1. Melt chocolate and peanut butter
1. Mix in marshmallows, peanuts and rice krispies
1. Drop by teaspoonful onto wax paper
