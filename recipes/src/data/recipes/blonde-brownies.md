# Blonde Brownies

- **Servings:** {36}
- **Yield:** {36} brownies

## Ingredients

- {2 1/4} cup All-purpose Flour
- {2 1/2} tsp Baking Powder
- {1/2} tsp Salt
- {1 3/4} cup Brown Sugar, firmly packed
- {3/4} cup Butter, softened
- {3} Egg
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {12} oz Chocolate Chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
1. Grease a 15x10-inch jelly-roll pan
1. Combine flour, baking powder and salt in a small bowl
1. Beat sugar and butter in a large mixing bowl until creamy
1. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract; gradually beat in flour mixture
1. Stir in morsels
1. Spread into prepared pan
1. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until top is golden brown
1. Cool in pan on wire rack
