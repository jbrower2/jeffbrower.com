# Boston Cream

- **Servings:** {6}
- **Yield:** {2} cups

## Ingredients

- {2} cup Milk
- {1/2} Vanilla Bean, split lengthwise and seeds scraped out
- {6} Egg Yolk
- {2/3} cup Sugar
- {1/4} cup Cornstarch
- {1} tbsp Butter

## Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan, heat the milk and vanilla bean to a boil over medium heat
1. Immediately turn off the heat and set aside to infuse for 10 to 15 minutes
1. In a bowl, whisk the egg yolks and granulated sugar until light and fluffy
1. Add the cornstarch and whisk vigorously until no lumps remain
1. Whisk in 1/4 cup of the hot milk mixture until incorporated
1. Whisk in the remaining hot milk mixture, reserving the empty saucepan
1. Pour the mixture through a strainer back into the saucepan
1. Cook over medium-high heat, whisking constantly, until thickened and slowly boiling
1. Remove from the heat and stir in the butter
1. Let cool slightly
1. Cover with plastic wrap, lightly pressing the plastic against the surface to prevent a skin from forming
1. Chill at least 2 hours or until ready to serve
