# Lime Sherbet

- **Servings:** {4}
- **Yield:** {4} cups

## Ingredients

- {1} cup Sugar
- {1} cup Water
- {2/3} cup Lime Juice
- {1} tbsp Lime Zest
- {2} cup Milk, whole
- {0} Food Coloring

## Instructions

1. Heat the granulated sugar and water in a small saucepan, stirring frequently, until the sugar has completely dissolved
1. This is a sugar syrup and it will help your sherbet have a smooth texture
1. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the lime juice and zest
1. Refrigerate the mixture until it is completely chilled
1. Stir the milk and green food coloring into the lime juice
1. Churn the sherbet according to the directions for your ice cream freezer
1. Store the lime sherbet in an air-tight plastic container in your freezer
1. Allowing it to set for at least an hour before freezing will give you a firmer texture than if you serve it right out of the ice cream maker
