# Peanut Butter Pie

- **Servings:** {8}
- **Yield:** {1} pie

## Ingredients

- {1} Pie Crust, baked
- {1/2} cup Peanut Butter
- {1} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {1/4} cup Milk
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {8} oz Cream Cheese
- {8} oz Whipped Topping

## Instructions

1. In large bowl combine all the ingredients except the toppings and crust
1. Blend with a hand mixer
1. Gently fold in whipped topping; pour into prepared crust and freeze for 2 hour
1. Once frozen, drizzle with ice cream topping and re-freeze for 20 minutes
1. Thaw slightly before serving and cut into thin slices because this is a rich pie
