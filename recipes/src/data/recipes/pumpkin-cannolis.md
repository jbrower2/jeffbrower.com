# Pumpkin Cannolis

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {12} cannolis

## Ingredients

- {2} tbsp Vanilla Extract
- {8} oz Mascarpone Cheese
- {3 1/2} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {3/4} cup Canned Pumpkin
- {2} cup Ricotta Cheese
- {2} tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice
- {12} Cannoli Shells

## Instructions

1. Place ricotta into a bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, and beat for 2-3 minutes until fluffy
1. Add Mascarpone cheese and continue beating on medium high speed until cheeses are blended together
1. Carefully add confectioners’ sugar and blend well
1. Add vanilla extract, pumpkin, and pie spice to cannoli filling and continue beating until mixture is smooth
1. Place a pastry bag fitted with a Wilton 6B tip into a large bar glass with tip end flipped upwards, resting on the bottom of the glass, and bag ends folded down over the sides of the glass
1. Using a spatula fill pastry bag with pumpkin ricotta cannoli filling
1. Fold each side of pastry bag in toward the center of bag and fold down top edges
1. Carefully lift bag from glass and twist bag pushing filling toward the tip
1. Place tip into one side of shell as close to the middle as possible and gently apply pressure to the bag to fill shell with Cannoli filling
1. Arrange filled cannoli shells on a serving platter and dust with reserved confectioners’ sugar
