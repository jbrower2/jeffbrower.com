# Chocolate Fudge

- **Servings:** {32}
- **Yield:** {64} pieces

## Ingredients

- {2 3/4} cup Sugar
- {4} oz Unsweetened Chocolate
- {3} tbsp Butter
- {1} cup Half-and-half Cream
- {1} tbsp Light Corn Syrup
- {1} tbsp Vanilla Extract
- {1} cup Nuts, optional

## Instructions

1. Grease an 8 by 8-inch pan with butter
1. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, combine the sugar, chocolate, 1 1/2 tablespoons of the butter, half-and-half, and corn syrup
1. Over medium heat, stir with a wooden spoon until sugar is dissolved and chocolate is melted
1. Increase heat and bring to a boil
1. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and boil for 3 minutes
1. Remove the cover and attach a candy thermometer to the pot
1. Cook until the thermometer reads 234 degrees F
1. Remove from the heat and add the remaining butter
1. Do not stir
1. Let the mixture cool for 10 minutes or until it drops to 130 degrees F
1. Add vanilla and nuts, if desired, and mix until well-blended and the shiny texture becomes matte
1. Pour into the prepared pan
1. Let sit in cool dry area until firm
1. Cut into 1-inch pieces and store in an airtight container for up to a week
