# Chocolate Donuts

- **Servings:** {10}
- **Yield:** {10} donuts

## Ingredients

- {1} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1/2} cup Sugar
- {1/4} cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
- {1/4} cup Mini Chocolate Chips
- {1/2} tsp Baking Soda
- {1/2} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {1} Egg
- {6} tbsp Sour Cream
- {1/4} cup Milk
- {1/4} cup Vegetable Oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
1. In a medium mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, chocolate chips, and baking soda
1. In a small bowl, beat together the vanilla, egg, sour cream, milk, and oil
1. Stir the wet ingredients into the dry until just combined
1. Spoon in a greased donut pan
1. Bake for 8 minutes or until the tops spring back when you touch them
1. Let the donuts cool in the pan before glazing
