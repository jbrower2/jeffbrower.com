# Chocolate Chip/Cinnamon Pizzelles

- **Servings:** {5}
- **Yield:** {20} pizzelles

## Ingredients

- {2/3} cup Sugar
- {4} Egg White, at room temperature
- {2} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {1/2} tsp Salt
- {1/2} stick Butter, melted
- {2/3} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1} tsp Cinnamon
- {1/2} cup Mini Chocolate Chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat the pizzelle iron to medium-high heat
1. Whisk the sugar, egg whites, vanilla, and salt in a large bowl until frothy
1. Whisk in the butter, flour, and cinnamon until blended
1. Stir in the chocolate chips
1. Brush the pizzelle cooking surfaces with melted butter
1. Spoon 1 tablespoon of batter in the center of the pizzelle iron
1. Close the iron and cook until deep golden, about 3 minutes
1. Transfer the pizzelles to a cooling rack
1. Cool completely
1. Store the pizzelles airtight at room temperature
