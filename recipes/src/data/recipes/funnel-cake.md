# Funnel Cake

- **Servings:** {6}
- **Yield:** {6} servings

## Ingredients

- {1} qt Canola Oil
- {3} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1/4} cup Sugar
- {2} tsp Baking Powder
- {1/2} tsp Salt
- {3} Egg
- {2} cup Milk
- {1/2} cup Confectioners' Sugar, for sprinkling

## Instructions

1. Heat canola oil in deep-fryer to 350 degrees F
1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
1. Whisk in eggs and milk
1. (Use 1/2 cup of batter for each funnel cake.) Pour 1/2 cup batter through funnel into hot oil, moving in a circular motion as you do so, to make each spiral-shaped funnel cake
1. Fry each spiral of batter until golden brown, about 2 or 3 minutes, remove with tongs and lay on paper toweling to absorb oil
1. Transfer to a plate and sprinkle powdered sugar through a sieve onto top of hot dough
1. Serve immediately
