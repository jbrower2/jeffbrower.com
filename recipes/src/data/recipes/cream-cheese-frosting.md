# Cream Cheese Frosting

- **Servings:** {24}
- **Yield:** {3} cups

## Ingredients

- {1} lb Cream Cheese
- {2} stick Butter, softened
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract, softened
- {4} cup Confectioners' Sugar, sifted

## Instructions

1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, butter and vanilla together until smooth
1. Add the sugar and on low speed, beat until incorporated
1. Increase the speed to high and mix until very light and fluffy
