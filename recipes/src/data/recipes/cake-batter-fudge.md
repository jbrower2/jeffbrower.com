# Cake Batter Fudge

- **Servings:** {32}
- **Yield:** {64} pieces

## Ingredients

- {14} oz Condensed Milk
- {3 1/2} cup White Chocolate
- {3} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {1/2} tsp Almond Extract

## Instructions

1. Pour milk and white chocolate into a microwave-safe bowl
1. Heat for 2-3 minutes, or until white chocolate is almost completely melted
1. DO NOT OVERHEAT
1. Stir until completely blended, melted, and smooth
1. Immediately add vanilla and almond extract and combine thoroughly
1. Add a handful or so of rainbow sprinkles and fold in quickly because they will melt (and if they are stirred for too long they’ll turn the fudge an ugly gray color)
1. Transfer to an aluminum-foil lined or well-greased 8×8 inch baking pan for very thick fudge, or a 11X7 inch pan (this is the size I used and it worked perfectly)
1. Let set at room temperature or in the refrigerator
1. Once set, cut into cubes (peel off the foil if you used it!)
1. Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge
