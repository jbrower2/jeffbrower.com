# Pumpkin Pie

- **Servings:** {8}
- **Yield:** {1} pie

## Ingredients

- {8} oz Cream Cheese
- {2} cup Canned Pumpkin
- {1} cup Sugar
- {1/4} tsp Salt
- {1} Egg, slightly beaten
- {2} Egg Yolk, slightly beaten
- {1} cup Half-and-half Cream
- {1/2} stick Butter, melted
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {1/2} tsp Cinnamon
- {1/4} tsp Ginger
- {1} Pie Crust, unbaked

## Instructions

1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese with a hand mixer
1. Add the pumpkin and beat until combined
1. Add the sugar and salt, and beat until combined
1. Add the eggs mixed with the yolks, half-and-half, and melted butter, and beat until combined
1. Finally, add the vanilla, cinnamon, and ginger, if using, and beat until incorporated
1. Pour the filling into the warm prepared pie crust and bake for 50 minutes, or until the center is set
1. Place the pie on a wire rack and cool to room temperature
1. Cut into slices and top each piece with a generous amount of whipped cream
