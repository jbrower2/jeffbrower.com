# Mint Chocolate Fudge

- **Servings:** {32}
- **Yield:** {64} pieces

## Ingredients

- {2 1/2} cup Semisweet Chocolate
- {14} oz Condensed Milk
- {1 1/2} cup White Chocolate, chopped
- {1} tsp Peppermint Flavoring
- {1} tsp Shortening

## Instructions

1. Line an 8x8 baking pan with parchment paper
1. Spray paper with non-stick spray and set aside
1. In a small saucepan over low heat, melt 1.5 cups chocolate chips with 3/4 cup sweetened condensed milk, stirring occasionally
1. Spread melted chocolate mixture into pan and chill for 5-10 minutes
1. In another saucepan, melt white baking chips and remaining condensed milk, stirring until completely smooth
1. Remove from the heat and add peppermint extract and food coloring
1. Spread mint layer over chilled chocolate layer
1. Chill for 5-10 minutes as you make the final layer
1. Melt remaining 1 cup of chocolate chips with 1 tsp shortening in the microwave in 30 second increments
1. Stir until completely smooth
1. Spread over chilled mint layer
1. Refrigerate at least 2 hours or until fudge is completely set (could take up to 4 hours)
1. Cut into squares
1. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator up to two weeks
1. Fudge will freeze and thaw well
