# Oatmeal Raisin Cookie Pie

Created by Donna Fumuso.

- **Servings:** {8}
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20151206024802/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
- **Bake Time:** 45 minutes

## Ingredients

- {1} refrigerated pie pastry
- {3} eggs
- {3/4} cup light corn syrup
- {1/2} cup packed brown sugar
- {1/2} cup butter, melted
- {1} teaspoon vanilla extract
- {1/8} teaspoon salt
- {1} package (6 ounces) Girl Scout Rah-Rah Raisins™ cookies, crushed
- {1-1/2} cups raisins
- {1/4} cup quick-cooking oats
- Whipped cream, optional

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°. Unroll pastry sheet into a 9-in. pie plate; flute edge.
1. In a large bowl, beat eggs, corn syrup, brown sugar, melted butter, vanilla and salt until blended. Stir in crushed Rah-Rah Raisins™ cookies, raisins and oats. Pour into pie pastry.
1. Bake 45-55 minutes or until filling is set and top is golden brown. Cool on a wire rack; serve or refrigerate within 2 hours. If desired, serve with whipped cream.
