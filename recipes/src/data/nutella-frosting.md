# Nutella Frosting

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {2} cups

## Ingredients

- {1} cup Butter
- {2} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {3/4} cup Nutella
- {3} tbsp Heavy Cream
- {2} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {1/4} tsp Salt

## Instructions

1. Beat softened butter on medium speed with an electric or stand mixer
1. Beat for 2-3 minutes until smooth and creamy
1. You want a very creamy base before adding anything else
1. See my extensive notes about this in the post above
1. Add 2 cups of powdered sugar and continue to beat on medium speed
1. The mixture will be fairly thick
1. Add the Nutella and continue to beat on medium speed
1. Add 3 Tablespoons of heavy cream and the vanilla extract
1. If the frosting is too thick, add more heavy cream (1 Tablespoon measurements at a time)
1. If the frosting is too thin, add more powdered sugar (1/4 cup measurements at a time)
1. Taste the frosting and add salt to cut the sweetness
1. I added about 1/4 teaspoon of salt to mine
