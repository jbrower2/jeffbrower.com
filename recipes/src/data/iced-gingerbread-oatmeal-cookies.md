# Iced Gingerbread Oatmeal Cookies

Submitted by: Julia - [Source](https://www.tiktok.com/@homemadebynatalie/video/7581207276246027551)

- **Servings:** {22}
- **Yield:** {22} cookies
- **Chill Time:** 30 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 9-11 minutes

## Ingredients

- {1} cup unsalted butter, melted
- {1} cup brown sugar
- {1/2} cup granulated sugar
- {1} egg + {1} egg yolk
- {1} Tbsp vanilla extract
- {2} Tbsp molasses
- {1 1/2} cups old fashioned oats, blended to break up the oats (not blended into flour)
- {2} cups all purpose flour
- {2} tsp cinnamon
- {2} tsp ginger
- {1/4} tsp nutmeg
- {3/4} tsp salt
- {1/2} tsp baking soda
- {1/2} tsp baking powder

### Glaze

- {1 1/2} cups powdered sugar
- {2}-{3} Tbsp milk
- {1/2} tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Whisk together the melted butter and sugars, then whisk in the rest of the wet ingredients.
1. Fold in the dry ingredients.
1. Scoop with a 2 tablespoon cookie scoop and chill for 30 minutes.
1. Bake at 350°F for 9-11 minutes.
1. Once cool, dip in glaze and let set at least an hour.
