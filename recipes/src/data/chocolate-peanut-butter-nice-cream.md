# Chocolate Peanut Butter Soft Serve (Nice Cream!)

This is my go-to ice cream – made from bananas, peanut butter and cocoa powder. That's it.

- **Servings:** {2}
- [Source](https://nourishedbycaroline.ca/chocolate-peanut-butter-soft-serve-nice-cream/)
- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes
- **Total Time:** 5 minutes

## Ingredients

- {4} large bananas, frozen
- {2} tablespoons peanut butter
- {2 1/2} tablespoons cocoa powder, unsweetened

### Suggested Toppings

- more peanut butter
- cocoa nibs
- chocolate or carob chips (or drizzle)
- shredded coconut
- fresh berries

## Instructions

1. Slice your bananas and freeze overnight or for at least 12 hours.
1. Add your frozen bananas, peanut butter and cocoa powder in a food processor and process until smooth.
1. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container in the freezer.

## Notes

- **Peanut butter:** I use organic salted peanut butter, but you can use any kind you have on hand. You can also use sunflower seed butter for a nut-free version or almond butter if preferred.
