# Kickin' Cajun Seasoning Mix

A homemade Cajun spice blend for seasoning fish, chicken, or vegetables — you control the heat by adjusting the cayenne.

- **Servings:** {1}
- **Yield:** {1} batch, about {2 1/2} tablespoons
- [Source](https://www.fromvalerieskitchen.com/blackened-mahi-mahi/)

## Ingredients

- {1 1/2} teaspoons onion powder
- {1} teaspoon garlic powder
- {1} teaspoon smoked paprika
- {1} teaspoon dried thyme leaves
- {1} teaspoon dried oregano leaves
- {1} teaspoon fine grain sea salt
- {1/2} teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- {1/2} teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a small bowl.
