# Pumpkin Whoopie Pies

- **Servings:** {36}
- **Yield:** {36} pies

## Ingredients

- {2} cup Brown Sugar
- {1} cup Vegetable Oil
- {1 1/2} cup Canned Pumpkin
- {2} Egg
- {3} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1} tsp Salt
- {1} tsp Baking Powder
- {1} tsp Baking Soda
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {1 1/2} tbsp Cinnamon
- {1/2} tbsp Ginger
- {1/2} tbsp Cloves

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
1. Lightly grease baking sheets
1. Combine the oil and brown sugar
1. Mix in the pumpkin and eggs, beating well
1. Add the flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, 1 teaspoon vanilla, cinnamon, ginger and cloves
1. Mix well
1. Drop dough by heaping teaspoons onto the prepared baking sheets
1. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 10 to 12 minutes
1. Let cookies cool then make sandwiches from two cookies filled with Whoopie Pie Filling
