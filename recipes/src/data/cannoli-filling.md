# Cannoli Filling

- **Servings:** {14}
- **Yield:** {14} cannolis

## Ingredients

- {1/2} cup Heavy Cream
- {1} lb Ricotta Cheese
- {1/2} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {2} tsp Liqueur, nut-flavored
- {1/4} cup Date, finely chopped
- {1/2} cup Semisweet Chocolate, melted
- {1/4} cup Pistachio, chopped

## Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl whip the cream
1. Fold in the ricotta with a rubber spatula, working until creamy
1. Fold in the sugar, nut liqueur, and dates
1. Place 1 cannoli shell on a flat surface
1. Working from either end, fill the cannoli with the ricotta filling (using a pastry bag, if desired), pressing gently to ensure that the middle is filled
1. Dip both ends in the melted chocolate, then into the pistachios and place on a waxed paper lined baking sheet
1. Repeat with the remaining cannoli, and serve
