# Raspberry Cheesecake Flavoring

- **Servings:** {16}
- **Yield:** {1} cheesecake

## Ingredients

- {10} oz Raspberries, frozen
- {2} tbsp Sugar
- {2} tsp Cornstarch
- {1/2} cup Water

## Instructions

1. In a saucepan, combine raspberries, 2 tablespoons sugar, cornstarch, and water
1. Bring to boil, and continue boiling 5 minutes, or until sauce is thick
1. Strain sauce through a mesh strainer to remove seeds
1. Stir into batter
