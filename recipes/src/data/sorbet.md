# Sorbet

- **Servings:** {16}
- **Yield:** {2} qts

## Ingredients

- {3} cup Water
- {1} cup Sugar

## Instructions

1. Bring 3 cups water and sugar just to a boil in a medium saucepan over high heat, stirring until sugar dissolves
1. Remove from heat
1. Cool
1. Process sugar syrup and fruit\*, in batches, in a blender or food processor until smooth
1. If recipe calls for fruit juice, add it here
1. Cover and chill 2 hours
1. Pour mixture into the freezer container of a 1-gallon ice-cream maker, and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions
