# Chocolate Cinnamon Fudge

- **Servings:** {5}
- **Yield:** {5} servings

## Ingredients

- {14} oz Condensed Milk
- {2} tsp Cinnamon
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {1} lb Bittersweet Chocolate, chopped/chips
- {3} tbsp Butter

## Instructions

1. Butter the bottom and sides of an 8 by 8-inch baking pan
1. Line the pan with a sheet of parchment paper, about 14-inches long and 7-inches wide, allowing the excess to overhang the sides
1. Set aside
1. In a medium glass or stainless steel bowl, combine the condensed milk, cinnamon, and vanilla
1. Stir in the chocolate chips and butter
1. Put the bowl on a saucepan of barely simmering water and mix until the chocolate chips have melted and the mixture is smooth, about 6 to 8 minutes (mixture will be thick)
1. Using a spatula, scrape the mixture into the prepared pan and smooth the top
1. Sprinkle with salt, if desired
1. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours until firm
1. Run a warm knife around the edge of the pan to loosen the fudge
1. Remove the fudge to a cutting board
1. Peel off the parchment paper and cut the fudge into 1-inch pieces
1. Store refrigerated in an airtight container or freeze
