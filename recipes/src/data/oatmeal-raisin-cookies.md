# Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

- **Servings:** {60}
- **Yield:** {60} cookies

## Ingredients

- {1} stick Butter, softened
- {1/2} cup Shortening, softened
- {1 1/2} cup Brown Sugar, packed
- {1} Egg
- {1/2} cup Buttermilk
- {1 3/4} cup All-purpose Flour
- {1} tsp Baking Soda
- {1/2} tsp Salt
- {1} tsp Baking Powder
- {1} tsp Ginger
- {1} tsp Nutmeg
- {1} tsp Cinnamon
- {1/4} tsp Cloves
- {1/2} tsp Allspice
- {2 1/2} cup Oatmeal
- {1} cup Raisins
- {1 1/2} cup Walnuts, chopped
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract

## Instructions

1. Grease 1 or more cookie sheets
1. Using an electric mixer, cream together butter, shortening, and sugar in a bowl until fluffy
1. Add eggs and beat until mixture is light in color
1. Add buttermilk
1. Sift together flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder, ginger, nutmeg, cinnamon, cloves, and allspice; stir into creamed mixture
1. Fold in oatmeal, raisins, walnuts, and vanilla, blending well
1. Drop by rounded teaspoons onto cookie sheet
1. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes
1. Drizzle with Brown Butter Icing
