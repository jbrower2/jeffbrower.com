# Thin Mints® Cupcakes

Created by Crystal Oliver.

- **Servings:** {24}
- **Yield:** {2} dozen cupcakes
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 40 minutes
- **Bake Time:** 15 minutes

## Ingredients

- {2} packages (9 ounces each) Girl Scout Thin Mints® cookies, divided
- {1} package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
- {1} package chocolate cake mix (regular size)

### Ganache

- {4} ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
- {1/2} cup heavy whipping cream
- {1/4} teaspoon peppermint extract

### Frosting

- {1} cup butter, softened
- {1} cup shortening
- {1} teaspoon vanilla or almond extract
- {1/2} teaspoon salt
- {3/4} cup baking cocoa
- {1/2} cup 2% milk
- {4} to {4-1/2} cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°. Reserve {6} Thin Mints® cookies for garnish. For truffles, place remaining Thin Mints® cookies in a food processor; process until finely crushed. Add cream cheese; process until blended. Roll into {24} 1-1/2-in. balls.
1. Line 24 muffin cups with paper liners. Prepare cake mix batter according to package directions. Drop 1 tablespoon batter into prepared muffin cups. Place one truffle in center of cup. Fill two-thirds full with remaining batter.
1. Bake 14-18 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the cake portion comes out clean. Cool in pans 10 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
1. Place chocolate in a small bowl. In a small saucepan, bring cream just to a boil. Pour over chocolate; let stand 5 minutes or until chocolate is melted. Stir with a whisk until smooth. Stir in peppermint extract. Cool ganache 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
1. For frosting, in a large bowl, beat butter, shortening, vanilla and salt until creamy. Beat in baking cocoa and milk alternately with enough confectioners' sugar to reach spreading consistency. Frost cupcakes; drizzle with ganache. Garnish with reserved Thin Mints® cookies.
