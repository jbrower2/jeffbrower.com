# Pumpkin Drop Cookies

- **Servings:** {48}
- **Yield:** {48} cookies

## Ingredients

- {1/2} cup Butter, softened
- {3/4} cup Sugar
- {3/4} cup Brown Sugar, packed
- {2} Egg
- {15} oz Canned Pumpkin
- {2 1/2} cup All-purpose Flour
- {2 1/2} tsp Baking Powder
- {1} tsp Baking Soda
- {1} tsp Salt
- {1} tsp Cinnamon
- {1} tsp Allspice
- {1} tsp Nutmeg
- {1} cup Raisins

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375º
1. Grease cookie sheet with shortening
1. Mix butter and sugars in large bowl with spoon
1. Beat in eggs
1. Stir in pumpkin
1. Stir in remaining ingredients except raisins
1. Fold in raisins
1. Drop dough by tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto cookie sheet
1. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until set and golden
1. Cool 1 to 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheet to wire rack
