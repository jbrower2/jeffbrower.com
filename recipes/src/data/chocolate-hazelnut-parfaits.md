# Chocolate Hazelnut Parfaits

- **Servings:** {8}
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Chill Time:** 1 hour

## Ingredients

- {3} cups cold milk
- {1} cup refrigerated hazelnut nondairy creamer
- {2} packages (3.9 ounces each) instant chocolate pudding mix
- {1} cup crushed Shortbread/Trefoil® Girl Scout Cookies
- {2} cups sliced fresh strawberries
- Whipped cream, optional

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, whisk the milk, creamer, and pudding mixes for 2 minutes. Let stand for 2 minutes or until soft-set.
1. Spoon 1/4 cup pudding into each of eight parfait glasses; sprinkle each with 1 tablespoon cookie crumbs. Top with strawberries and remaining pudding and crumbs. Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving. Garnish with whipped cream if desired.
