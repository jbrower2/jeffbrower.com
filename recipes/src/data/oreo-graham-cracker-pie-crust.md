# Oreo/Graham Cracker Pie Crust

- **Servings:** {1}
- **Yield:** {1} pie crust

## Ingredients

- {2} cup Graham Crackers, crumbs
- {1 1/2} cup Oreos, sandwich crumbs
- {1/4} cup Sugar
- {1} stick Butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
1. In the bowl of a food processor or blender, process the graham crackers and chocolate cookies to make crumbs
1. In a bowl mix together the cookie crumbs, sugar and butter with your hands
1. Press the mixture evenly into a 9-inch pie pan
1. (It will seem like an excessive amount of crumbs, but will compress down quite a bit with pressure.) Cover the crust with plastic wrap and top with another 9-inch pan, pressing quite firmly to make a smooth surface
1. Remove the pan and the plastic wrap from the pressed crust prior to placing it into the preheated oven
1. Bake the crust for 15 minutes
1. Remove the crust from the oven and let cool completely before filling
1. To make a graham cracker crust, omit Oreo crumbs and use 3 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
