# Orange Cream Cheese Frosting

- **Servings:** {18}
- **Yield:** One 8-inch 3-layer cake or {36} cupcakes
- [Source](https://www.food.com/recipe/orange-cream-cheese-frosting-466179)
- **Total Time:** 15 minutes

## Ingredients

- {1} (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- {1/4} cup unsalted butter, softened
- {1} tablespoon fresh orange juice
- {1} teaspoon grated orange zest
- {1/2} teaspoon pure vanilla extract
- {4} cups confectioners' sugar
- {1/4} teaspoon orange food coloring

## Instructions

1. Blend the cream cheese, butter, orange juice, orange zest, and vanilla in a large bowl, using an electric mixer set at medium speed, until smooth.
1. Add the sugar and continue to beat until light and creamy — about 3 more minutes.
1. Add the food coloring and gently stir until the color is uniform.
1. Chill the frosting until ready to ice the cupcakes or cake.
