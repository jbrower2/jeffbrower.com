# Chili

Recipe by: Jeff

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Cook Time:** 8 hours

## Ingredients

- {2} lbs ground beef
- {1} lb thick cut bacon, finely chopped
- {1} lb sweet sausage
- {45} oz tomato sauce
- {30} oz diced tomatoes
- {30} oz kidney beans (drained)
- {15} oz pinto beans (drained)
- {15} oz black beans (drained)
- {1} pepper diced green bell pepper
- {1} pepper diced red bell pepper
- {1 1/2} medium onions, diced
- {3} cloves minced garlic
- {2} tbsp chili powder
- {2} tsp salt
- {1 1/2} tsp dried basil
- {1} tsp dried parsley
- {1} tsp dried oregano
- {1} tsp crushed red pepper flakes
- {1/2} tsp ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Fully cook the ground beef and sausage
1. Fry the bacon. I like to cut it up into ¼” strips first with a sharp knife
1. Cut up the produce
1. Drain the beans
1. Combine all ingredients and cook in a slow cooker on low for 8 hours (can also cook on the stove top: bring to a low boil on medium, then simmer on low for 60 minutes)
