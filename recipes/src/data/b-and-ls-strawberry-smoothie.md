# B and L's Strawberry Smoothie

This strawberry smoothie recipe blends strawberries with milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla for an icy-cold treat. You'll love it!

- **Servings:** {2}
- [Source](https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/20792/b-and-ls-strawberry-smoothie/)
- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes
- **Total Time:** 5 minutes

## Ingredients

- {8} strawberries, hulled
- {1/2} cup skim milk
- {1/2} cup plain yogurt
- {3} tablespoons white sugar
- {2} teaspoons vanilla extract
- {6} cubes ice, crushed

## Instructions

1. Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.
1. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
