# Marshmallow Frosting

- **Servings:** {30}
- **Yield:** {30} cupcakes worth

## Ingredients

- {3} cup Heavy Cream
- {1 1/2} cup Marshmallow Cream

## Instructions

1. Using electric mixer and large bowl, whisk whipping cream and marshmallow cream on low and gradually move up to high as it thicken - mix until stiff peaks form
