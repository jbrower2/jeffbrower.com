# Chocolate Peanut Butter Frosting

- **Servings:** {1}
- **Yield:** {1} batch, about {1 2/3} cups
- [Source](https://www.tasteofhome.com/recipes/chocolate-peanut-butter-frosting/)
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Ingredients

- {1/3} cup butter, softened
- {1/3} cup creamy peanut butter
- {1 1/2} teaspoons vanilla extract
- {2 1/2} cups confectioners' sugar
- {1/3} cup baking cocoa
- {1/4} teaspoon salt
- {4} to {5} tablespoons 2% milk

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, cream butter and peanut butter until light and fluffy. Beat in vanilla. Stir in the confectioners' sugar, cocoa, salt and enough milk to achieve a spreading consistency.
