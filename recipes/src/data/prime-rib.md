# Prime Rib

- **Servings:** {12}
- **Yield:** {12} servings

## Ingredients

- {10} lb Prime Rib, bone-in
- {1} stick Butter, melted
- {10} Garlic Cloves
- {1 1/2} tbsp Oregano
- {1} tbsp Thyme
- {1} tbsp Rosemary
- {1 1/2} tbsp Salt
- {2} tsp Black Pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine all seasoning ingredients (butter, garlic, oregano, thyme, rosemary, salt, and black pepper) in a small bowl
1. Remove the prime rib from the fridge and season it
1. Let sit for 45 minutes before cooking
1. Place in a roasting pan on a roasting rack, fat side up
1. Cook at 450 for 20 minutes, then reduce to 325
1. Cook until internal temperature reaches 120
1. Let rest for 15 minutes before serving — internal temperature will continue rising to about 130
