# Butter Pecan Fudge

- **Servings:** {32}
- **Yield:** {64} pieces

## Ingredients

- {1} stick Butter
- {1/2} cup Sugar
- {1/2} cup Brown Sugar
- {1/2} cup Heavy Cream
- {1/8} tsp Salt
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {2} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {1} cup Pecans, chopped

## Instructions

1. In a large heavy saucepan, combine the butter, sugars, cream and salt
1. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally
1. Boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly
1. Remove from the heat; stir in vanilla
1. Stir in confectioners' sugar until smooth
1. Fold in pecans
1. Spread into a buttered 8-in
1. square dish
1. Cool to room temperature
1. Cut into 1-in
1. squares
1. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator
1. Yield: 1 1/4 pounds
