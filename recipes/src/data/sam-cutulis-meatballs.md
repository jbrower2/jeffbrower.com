# Sam Cutuli's Meatballs

- **Servings:** {4}

## Ingredients

- {1} lb ground meat
- {1} cup Italian style breadcrumbs
- {1/2} cup grated Parmesan cheese
- {2} large eggs
- {1/2} red onion, finely chopped
- Small amount of salt and pepper to taste
- Handful of fresh parsley and basil, cut up
- {1/8} cup water (a little more if you think it needs it)

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
1. Form into meatballs and place on a tray lined with parchment paper.
1. Bake at 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes.

## Notes

- **Meatloaf variation:** Can also make this recipe into a meatloaf. Place on a tray lined with parchment paper and bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
