# Copycat Chick-fil-A Lemonade

This Copycat Chick-fil-A Lemonade is just what you need to quench your summertime thirst! Super easy, it's a sweet 'n tart drink that is so refreshing!

- **Servings:** {4}
- [Source](https://therecipecritic.com/copycat-chick-fil-lemonade/)
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Chill Time:** 1 hour
- **Total Time:** 1 hour 10 minutes

## Ingredients

- {1 3/4} cup lemon juice, about 7–8 large lemons, freshly squeezed
- {1} cup sugar
- {5} cups cold water

## Instructions

1. In a large pitcher, mix sugar and lemon juice until blended. Add the cold water and mix. Chill in the fridge for at least an hour. Enjoy!
