# Mile-High Peppermint Pie

- **Servings:** {8}
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 40 minutes
- **Bake Time:** 5 minutes
- **Freeze Time:** several hours

## Ingredients

- {1 1/2} cups crushed Thin Mints® Girl Scout Cookies (about {24} cookies)
- {2} tablespoons butter, melted
- {5} cups peppermint ice cream, softened if necessary

### Chocolate Sauce

- {1} ounce semisweet chocolate
- {1} tablespoon butter
- {1/2} cup sugar
- {3} tablespoons water
- {2} tablespoons light corn syrup
- {1/8} teaspoon salt
- {1/2} teaspoon vanilla extract

### Meringue

- {5} large egg whites, room temperature
- {2/3} cup sugar

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix crushed cookies and melted butter. Press onto the bottom and up sides of a 9-in. metal pie pan. Freeze, covered, for 1 hour or until firm.
1. Spread ice cream into crust. Freeze, covered, until firm.
1. To serve, preheat oven to 450°. In a small saucepan, melt chocolate and butter over medium-low heat. Stir in sugar, water, corn syrup, and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring to dissolve sugar. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, 4–5 minutes or until slightly thickened, stirring frequently. Stir in vanilla; keep warm.
1. For meringue, in a small, heavy saucepan, combine egg whites and sugar. Using a hand mixer, beat on low speed 1 minute. Place saucepan over low heat; continue beating on low until a thermometer reads 160°.
1. Transfer mixture to a bowl. Beat on high until stiff glossy peaks form. Spread over frozen pie. Bake 2–3 minutes or until meringue is lightly browned. Serve immediately with warm chocolate sauce.
