# Watermelon Pie

- **Servings:** {8}
- **Yield:** {1} pie

## Ingredients

- {2} cup Watermelon Rind, chopped
- {1} cup Sugar
- {2} tsp All-purpose Flour
- {1} tsp Cinnamon
- {1/4} tsp Nutmeg
- {1/4} tsp Cloves
- {1/8} tsp Salt
- {3} tbsp Cider Vinegar
- {1/2} cup Raisins
- {1/2} cup Pecans
- {2} Pie Crust, unbaked

## Instructions

1. Place watermelon rind in a small saucepan; add water to cover
1. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer for about 20 minutes or until tender
1. Remove from heat; drain
1. Combine watermelon rind with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, salt, vinegar, raisins, and pecans; stir to combine then set aside
1. Fit 1 pie crust into a 9-inch pie plate
1. Pour watermelon rind mixture into the pie crust
1. Top with remaining pie crust; fold edges under, and crimp all around the edge
1. Cut small slits in top crust
1. Bake at 350° for 45 to 50 minutes, or until golden, shielding edges of pie with strips of aluminum foil after 25 minutes to prevent excessive browning
1. Cool watermelon pie on a wire rack
