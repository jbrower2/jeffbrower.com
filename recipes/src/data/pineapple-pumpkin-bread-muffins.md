# Pineapple Pumpkin Bread/Muffins

- **Servings:** {18}
- **Yield:** {18} muffins/1 loaf

## Ingredients

- {1} cup Sugar
- {1/2} cup Shortening
- {1} cup Canned Pumpkin
- {2} Egg
- {2 1/2} cup All-purpose Flour
- {2 1/2} tsp Baking Soda
- {1/2} tsp Salt
- {1/2} cup Pineapple, dried/chopped

## Instructions

1. Grease muffin tins
1. Preheat oven 350 degrees F
1. In medium bowl, cream sugar and shortening
1. Stir in pumpkin and eggs
1. In separate bowl, mix together the dry ingredients: flour, baking soda, and salt
1. Add dry ingredients to pumpkin mix
1. Quickly stir ingredients until flour is wet
1. Fold in pineapple
1. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full with batter
1. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 to 25 minutes or until done
1. Remove muffins from tin and allow to cool
1. For bread: fill a buttered loaf pan with batter and bake at 325 degrees F for 1 hour
