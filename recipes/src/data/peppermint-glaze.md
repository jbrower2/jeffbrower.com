# Peppermint Glaze

- **Servings:** {100}
- **Yield:** {200} cookies worth

## Ingredients

- {6} cup Confectioners' Sugar
- {1/8} tsp Mint Extract
- {1/2} cup Water

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl mix together the sugar; mint extract and water until smooth
1. Transfer mixture to a medium resealable plastic bag and snip off the corner
