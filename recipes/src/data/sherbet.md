# Sherbet

- **Servings:** {16}
- **Yield:** {2} qt

## Ingredients

- {1} cup Sugar
- {2} cup Milk
- {2} cup Half-and-half Cream
- {1/4} oz Unflavored Gelatin

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and gelatin in a large bowl
1. Heat milk to almost boiling
1. Pour over sugar mixture and stir until dissolved
1. Stir in fruit mixture
1. Stir in light cream and food coloring until combined
1. Mixture may appear curdled
1. Cool to room temperature
1. Put mixture into ice cream maker and prepare according to manufacturer's directions
