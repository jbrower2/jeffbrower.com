# Rocky Road Fudge

- **Servings:** {64}
- **Yield:** {128} pieces

## Ingredients

- {12} oz Semisweet Chocolate, chopped
- {14} oz Condensed Milk
- {1} tsp Vanilla Extract
- {3} cup Mini Marshmallows
- {1 1/2} cup Peanuts

## Instructions

1. LINE 13 x 9-inch baking pan with foil; grease lightly
1. MICROWAVE morsels and sweetened condensed milk in large, microwave-safe bowl on HIGH (100 percent) power for 1 minute; stir
1. Microwave at additional 10- to 20-second intervals, stirring until smooth
1. Stir in vanilla extract
1. Fold in marshmallows and nuts
1. PRESS mixture into prepared baking pan
1. Refrigerate until ready to serve
1. Lift from pan; remove foil
1. Cut into pieces
