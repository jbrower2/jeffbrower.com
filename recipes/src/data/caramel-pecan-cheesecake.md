# Caramel Pecan Cheesecake

- **Servings:** {12}
- [Source](https://web.archive.org/web/20211026215212/https://www.girlscouts.org/en/cookies/all-about-cookies/Cookie-Recipes.html)
- **Prep Time:** 30 minutes
- **Bake Time:** 1 hour 15 minutes
- **Chill Time:** 8 hours

## Ingredients

- {2} cups crushed Shortbread/Trefoil® Girl Scout Cookies
- {3} tablespoons butter, melted
- {1/4} cup plus {2} tablespoons all-purpose flour, divided
- {1} jar (12 1/4 ounces) caramel ice cream topping
- {1} cup chopped pecans
- {5} packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
- {1 3/4} cups sugar
- {1 1/2} teaspoons vanilla extract
- {4} eggs
- {2} egg yolks
- {1/3} cup heavy whipping cream

### Sour Cream Topping

- {2} cups (8 ounces each) sour cream
- {1/3} cup sugar

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, combine cookie crumbs and butter. Press onto the bottom and 1 in. up the sides of a greased 10-in. springform pan. Place pan on a baking sheet. Bake at 350° for 8–10 minutes or until set. Cool on a wire rack.
1. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, stir {1/4} cup flour into the caramel topping. Set aside {1/3} cup caramel mixture and {2} tablespoons pecans for garnish. Drizzle remaining caramel mixture over crust; sprinkle with remaining pecans.
1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, and remaining flour until smooth. Beat in eggs and yolks just until combined. Stir in cream. Pour over crust. Bake at 325° for 65–70 minutes or until center is almost set.
1. In a small bowl, combine sour cream and sugar; carefully spread over warm filling. Bake 10–12 minutes longer or until topping is set. Cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Carefully run a knife around edge of pan to loosen; cool 1 hour longer.
1. Chill for 8 hours or overnight. Remove sides of pan. Just before serving, drizzle with reserved caramel mixture and sprinkle with reserved pecans. Refrigerate leftovers.
