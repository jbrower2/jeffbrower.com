Deck Optimization Report

Every deck was repeatedly proposed small, legal, on-theme card swaps and kept the ones that raised its win rate against the whole field. This is the full change log — each deck's before/after standing and exactly which swaps got it there.

Two numbers, on purpose. Gained is the win% a deck's own accepted swaps added (the sum of each change's measured lift) — this is improvement caused by the changes. Field (before → after) is its standing versus the whole field, which also shifted as every deck improved — so a deck whose swaps clearly helped can still show a lower field number if the rest of the field improved faster. Win% is measured over 164 opponents × 24 games each.
