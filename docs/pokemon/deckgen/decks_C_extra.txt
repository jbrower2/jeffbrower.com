- Premium: **Cyclizar ex** — Break Through 130 + bench snipe (colorless); Zircon Road 180 + draw 5 with a grass/fire/psychic splash.
- Supports: **Cinderace** (Turbo Flare loads 3 energy of any type) + **Magearna** (Auto Heal — 90 per attach).

**Terapagos Bench-Scale** — go wide, hit wide

- Supports: **Togekiss** (Wonder Kiss — coin-flip an extra prize per KO) + **Drednaw** (walls a 200+ hit).
